## About the Community

The **Suika3 Community** develops and maintains the Suika3 visual novel engine.
We are collective of developers and creators working together to build a 
sustainable ecosystem for visual novel development.

Our primary goal is to maintain and evolve **Suika3**, a high-performance
visual novel engine designed for professional production especially mobile
app stores and long-term support.

We welcome contributions from developers, writers, translators,
and creators from around the world.

If you are interested in shaping the future of visual novel technology,
we would love to have you with us.

## Core Repositories

The Suika3 ecosystem is composed of several key projects.

### Engine

* **[Suika3](https://github.com/suika3-community/suika3)**  
  The main visual novel engine built on Playfield Engine and NoctLang.

### Foundation Components

* **[PlayfieldEngine](https://github.com/awemorris/PlayfieldEngine)**  
  The native 2D engine that powers rendering, audio, and platform abstraction.

* **[NoctLang](https://github.com/awemorris/NoctLang)**  
  The scripting language used by Suika3.

### Historical Components

* **[Suika2](https://github.com/suika3-community/suika2-archive)**  
  The Suika2 visual novel engine.

* **[OpenNovel](https://github.com/suika3-community/opennovel-archive)**  
  The OpenNovel visual novel engine.
